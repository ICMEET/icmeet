Dear Authors,
Greetings from the organizing team of ICMEET-2021!

1. Schedule of parallel tracks for technical paper presentations is given below for your reference and also made available on website through the following link: https://www.icmeet.in/pages/program

2. You may locate the session allocated to your registered paper(s) in the schedule for online presentation. The Google Meet meeting-links to login to a session are made available as per the schedule.

3. Paper presentations have been allocated a 15 minutes slot (which includes 10-12 minutes of presentation by authors followed by 2-3 minutes given to Q/A).

4. You are therefore expected to limit your power-point presentation within the allocated time frame; thereby focusing on your contributory work done and the obtained results.

5. Kindly do not put requests for change of allotted presentation slots.

6. Looking forward to your participation in the conference. Other details regarding Inaugural function (Virtual), Keynotes, etc. will shortly follow.

7. Further, we are happy to inform you that based on the merit of paper and its subsequent presentation during the conference--"TEN BEST PAPER AWARDS" will be announced at the end of the conference.

8. Looking forward to your active participation in the conference. Other details regarding Inaugural function (Virtual), Keynotes, etc. will shortly follow.

Best Wishes
